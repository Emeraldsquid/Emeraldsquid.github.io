About

Welcome to SWildgooseDesigns! I’m Sharna Wildgoose, an artist based in the Derbyshire Dales. I specialise in painting creatures, both real and mythical. Whether it’s a commissioned pet portrait or a vibrant wildlife scene, each piece is hand-painted with love and attention to detail.

Commissions

I offer custom artwork tailored to your preferences. Whether it’s a cherished pet portrait, a wildlife piece, or a magical creature, I’d love to bring your vision to life. Get in touch to discuss your ideas.
