About

Welcome to SWildgooseDesigns! I’m Sharna Wildgoose, an artist based in the Derbyshire Dales. I specialize in painting creatures—both real and mythical—on canvas, boards, and wood. Whether it’s a commissioned pet portrait or a vibrant wildlife scene, each piece is hand-painted with love and attention to detail.
